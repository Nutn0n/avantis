Avantis Protocal

DeFi multi-asset trading platform

Invest in global assets via a single state-of-the-art investment platform

Synthetic with real asset back

In contrast to other synthetic asset platforms, all Avantis's synthetic assets (aAsset) minted in Avantis protocol will be backed 1:1 by the underlying assets. Investors do not need to mint assets with the over-collateralization mechanism. If the asset price is $100, they will just pay $100 to mint it.

Large universe of asset available

Unlike other synthetic asset platforms that support limited assets, Avantis supports a wide range of assets.

You can trade most stocks on major exchanges like NYSE, Nasdaq, Hong Kong Stock Exchange, Tokyo Stock Exchange, Shanghai, and Shenzhen Stock Exchange (H-share).

Commodities in Chicago Board of Trade, Comex, Nymex, Global ETFs, mutual funds And more assets will be added to our roadmap.

Extra yield from liquidity providing

Once investors have aAsset in their portfolio, they can provide aAsset liquidity to Avantis Dex to gain extra yield as they did on other Dex.

State-of-the-art robo-advisory platform

Unlike other Dex trading platforms with only basic trading tools, the Avantis trading platform comes with a comprehensive mobile robo-advisory platform. The robo-advisor will advise and assist on investments in all asset classes, set notifications, monitor market movement, and help manage portfolio risks 24/7.

Dual Exchanges Mechanism (DEX/CEX)

No worry about liquidity shortage, enjoy low trading slippage

Avantis protocol pools liquidity from real-world and DeFi-world together with our sophisticated liquidity pool mechanism. We connect DeFi liquidity with real-world liquidity, ensuring that all your trading will not have liquidity risk or high trading slippage as long as the real-world market is working fine.
